Dark theme:

Gives Discogs.com a darker look. It's easier on the eyes - especially at night.

Marketplace highlights:

This will highlight the media and sleeve conditons in the Marketplace. See at a glance which items are in the best condition.

Sort buttons:

Adds a 'Sort A-Z' button to the Genre, Style, Format, Counrty and Decade dialog boxes within the Explore page. It's easier to find the sub-categories you're looking for.

Please refresh the page for changes to take effect.
