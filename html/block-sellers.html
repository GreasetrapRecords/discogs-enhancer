Block Seller Settings

1. Enter the seller's username exactly as it appears on Discogs.

2. Click "Block Seller" to add the seller to the blocked list.

Please note: you must refresh your Discogs page for changes to take effect.

Block Seller

Sellers will be marked in red and tagged with "Blocked seller" but still visible in the Marketplace.

When selected, any seller that appears on this list will be hidden from view in the Marketplace AND on individual release pages.

When selected, sellers will be hidden when viewing Marketplace items but shown when viewing an individual release page. Sellers on an individual release page will still be visible, marked in red, and tagged with "Blocked seller".

Blocked Sellers

Click on a seller's name to remove the seller from the blocked list.

How To Backup Your Blocked Sellers List

Copy the text in the box below and save it some place safe. If you ever need to restore your list of blocked sellers, just paste it into the restore box and hit "Restore".

Restore Your Blocked Sellers List

Paste the text from your backed-up blocked sellers list here and click "Restore". Note: after restoring, you will need to choose whether you want to tag or hide the blocked sellers again.
